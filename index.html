The Energy And Environment Foundation (EEF) Global Hydrogen Awards are conferred on the highest achievers celebrating excellence in International HydrogenIndustry. The EEF Global Hydrogen Awards are designed to recognize the exceptional achievements from companies and individuals who are driving the hydrogen industry forward and award best initiatives in scientific management of Hydrogen, Industrial Hydrogen Units,Devices, Instrument Manufacturing Companies, Hydrogen Infrastructure, Hydrogen Storage, Hydrogen Mobility, Hydrogen Heavy Transport, Hydrogen Railways, Hydrogen Aviation, Shipping and Hydrogen Safety, across the globe in competing areas like Innovation Technology, Conservation and Sustainable Finance that has made an outstanding impact. The Global Awards are also focused in the areas of Hydrogen Fuel Cell / Electrolytes / Compressor Manufacturing Companies / Unit / Research / Safety Management / R&D / Academic Institutes.
The EEF Global Hydrogen Awards will be presented at the World Hydrogen Energy Summit 2021 (WHS21) for the Hydrogen Industry worldwide.

Nomination Shortlisting & Selection Process 

The EEF Global Hydrogen Awards shortlisting process is the procedure by which the pool of nominations submitted in the open nomination phase are examined by a panel of independent experts, who shortlist the entries and compile them into award categories. Shortlisting begins after nominations have closed.

All the entries received scrutinized for various award categories for EEF Global Hydrogen Awards 2021 will beexamined and selected by the eminent jury. The selected awards are announced online on November 1, 2021. Decisions of the eminent jury are final and will be binding on all applicants.

The winners of the EEF Global Hydrogen Awards will be announced and honoured at the Global Hydrogen Awards ceremony on November 17, 2021.
We wish everyone the best of luck!
